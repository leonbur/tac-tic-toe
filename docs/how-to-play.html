How to Play Tac-Tic-Toe

Objective

The goal is to win three small tic-tac-toe boards in a row like in the classic game to claim victory - on the big board.

Basic Rules

  1. The big board is a 3x3 grid of small tic-tac-toe boards.
  2. When a player plays in a cell of a small board, the index of that cell is index of the small board that the next player must play in.

  3. Win small tic-tac-toe boards to claim that space in the big board.
  4. If sent to a completed board, your opponent can play in any available board.


  5. Players alternate turns, starting with X.
  6. If a small board results in a draw, it is considered a neutral space.

Strategy Tips

  • Plan ahead and think about your opponent's possible moves.
  • Control the center board to increase your chances of winning.
  • Force your opponent into boards where you have an advantage.
